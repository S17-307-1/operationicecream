The system shall display the scores of the top 10 gamers for that day on a leaderboard screen.
The system shall increasingly become 5% more difficult for each level of the game.
The system shall provide a link to a set of instructions on the top left corner of the screen.
The system shall allow the user to save their progress by pressing the save button on the screen.
The system shall display the number of lives a user has left every time the player dies.
